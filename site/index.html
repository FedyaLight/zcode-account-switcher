ZCode Account Switcher

Save local ZCode login snapshots and switch between ZCode accounts on macOS without manually moving credential files.

Download for macOS View source

Built for macOS 13 or newer. Releases are distributed from GitHub.

Switch ZCode accounts from one native app.

ZCode Account Switcher stores each account as a local snapshot, shows basic account health, and restores the selected snapshot back into ZCode.

Local account snapshots

Capture the current ZCode login state and keep separate snapshots for different accounts.

One-click switching

Close ZCode, back up the active state, restore the selected account, and relaunch ZCode.

OAuth import flow

Add accounts through the supported OAuth capture path, with local import and export available.

Designed for the local ZCode account files.

The app works with the ZCode files under ~/.zcode/v2 and keeps saved snapshots in Application Support.

ZCode account switching FAQ.

Short answers for people looking for a ZCode account switcher on macOS.

How do I switch ZCode accounts on macOS?

Save each account as a snapshot in ZCode Account Switcher, then choose the account you want to restore before launching ZCode.

Where are ZCode account snapshots stored?

Snapshots are stored locally under ~/Library/Application Support/ZCode Account Switcher/accounts.

Is this affiliated with ZCode?

No. This is an independent native macOS implementation. ZCode and related marks belong to their respective owners.
